The Squeeze Theorem

The Squeeze Theorem provides another useful method for calculating limits. Suppose functions fff and hhh have the same limit LLL at a and assume the function ggg is trapped between fff and hhh. The Squeeze Theorem says that g must also have the limit LLL at aaa. A proof of this theorem is outlined in Exercise 54 of Section 2.7.
