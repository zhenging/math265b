Guideline

When testing a series of positive terms ∑ak\sum a_k∑ak​ for convergence

  1. Begin with The Divergence Test.
  2. Is the series a special series? Recall the convergence of the following series.
  • Geometric Series
  • p-Series
  • Check also for a telescoping series
  1. If the general kth term of the series looks like a function you can integrate, then try the Integral Test.
  2. If the general kth term of the series involves k!,kk,akk!, k^k, a^kk!,kk,ak, where aaa is a constant, the Ratio Test is advisable. Series with k in an exponent yield to the Root Test.
  3. If the general kth term of the series is a rational function of kkk (or a root of a rational function), Use the Direct Comparison Test or the Limit Comparison Test. (Use the families of series given in Step 2 as comparison series)
