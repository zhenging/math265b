31\. $\dlim_{x\to \infty}\dfrac{2x-3}{4x+10}$
>Solution
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{x\to \infty}\frac{2x-3}{4x+10} &= \lim_{x\to \infty}\dfrac{2-\frac{3}{x}}{4+\frac{10}{x}}\\
&= \frac{2-0}{4+0} = \frac{1}{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
