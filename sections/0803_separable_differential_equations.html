1. What is a separable first-order differential equation?

Solution

A separable first-order differential equation is one that can be written in the form g(y)y′(t)=h(t)g(y)y'(t) = h(t)g(y)y′(t)=h(t), where the factor g(y)g(y)g(y) is a function of yyy and h(t)h(t)h(t) is a function of ttt.
